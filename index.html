Farm Food's mission is to provide accessible and sustainable foods to our community through sustainable practices, reasonable prices and education.

We only practice with sustainable techniques.
We provide food that is accessible to everyone.
We offer education classes about health and food.

At Farm Food, we care about educating our community on growing food and nutrition! We offer an education series on nutrition that is completely free. There is a limited amount of tickets for each session. The event includes a free lunch and education materials to take home with you.
